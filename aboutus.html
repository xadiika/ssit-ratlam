ABOUT US

Shree Sai Institute of Technology (SSIT) was established by a registered society Ratlam Maa Sharda Uchcha Shiksha Samiti (RMSUSS), Ratlam.The college also runs the various courses provided by IGNOU. The college is located at a distance of nearly 2 kms from Ratlam bus/railway station. The institute has a newly constructed spacious building with beautiful surroundings within Ratlam Municipal Corporation limits, near R.T.O. office, at Jaora road. The institute has all modern educational facilities like spacious lecture halls, classrooms, library, well equipped computer lab. It also has enormous playground for games like cricket, football, volleyball and all athletics. SSIT has come up on its own land of several Bighas in natural surroundings. SSIT has one of the best infrastructures in Vikram University jurisdiction. It has well-furnished spacious classrooms and well-equipped computerized library. The classrooms as well as library have all modern audio-visual facilities like OHP, LCD etc
